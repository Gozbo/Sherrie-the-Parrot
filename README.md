Welcome aboard, Captain!

Sherrie the Parrot is the official first mate of the game. Sherrie keeps watch over your journey and provides quick access to game information, game features, and maritime updates. Expect nautical jokes, voyage announcements, and the occasional squawk from the most talkative bird on the sea.

About Sherrie

Legend says Sherrie once belonged to a legendary captain who sailed beyond the edge of every known map. After years of collecting stories from different kinds of sailors, she now shares her knowledge with anyone brave enough to answer the call of the sea.
